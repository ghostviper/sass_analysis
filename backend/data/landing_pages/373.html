Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ReplyHero

How does ReplyHero work?

ReplyHero is a Chrome Extension that generates AI-powered replies for LinkedIn and Twitter posts. Simply click on a post, choose your desired tone, and get 3 personalized reply options instantly.

What platforms does it support?

Currently, ReplyHero supports LinkedIn and Twitter (X). We're constantly working to add support for more social media platforms.

Can I customize the AI responses?

Yes! You can set your own personal context, choose from different tonalities (professional, casual, witty, etc.), and even customize the system prompt for more personalized responses.

Is there a free plan?

Yes, we offer a free plan with 1 daily replies for anonymous users and 2 daily replies for registered users. Upgrade to unlock unlimited replies and advanced features.

How authentic are the AI-generated replies?

Our AI is powered by expensive models and constantly tweaked and trained to generate human-like, contextually relevant responses. You can always edit the suggestions before posting to ensure they match your voice perfectly.
