Frequently Asked Questions

What is Saint, and how does it work?

Saint is a digital companion designed to support your Christian faith journey. It provides daily scripture insights, personalized prayer support, faith-based counseling, and a library of Bible stories. Simply open the app, explore the story library, submit your queries or prayers, and receive tailored responses grounded in Christian teachings.

Is Saint free to use?

Yes, Saint offers a free version with access to many of its core features. You can access the Bible stories library, receive daily scripture, prayer support, and guidance without any cost. For additional personalized features and premium content, a subscription option is available.

How do I start using Saint?

To start using Saint, simply click the button "Get Started". You will be directed to our platform, where you can begin your journey by following the on-screen prompts and exploring our Bible stories library.

Is Saint a replacement for a pastor or church?

No, Saint is not intended to replace a pastor or your local church. It serves as a supplemental tool to enhance your spiritual journey by offering daily support, guidance, and access to Bible stories. For deeper spiritual growth, it's recommended to stay connected with your church community and seek guidance from ordained pastors.

How secure is my information with Saint?

Your privacy is a top priority for us. Saint takes measures to protect your personal information. The messages you send are processed securely and are not stored permanently. We use industry-standard encryption to ensure the safety of your data.

Can I share Saint with my friends or family?

Yes! We encourage you to share Saint with others who may benefit from spiritual support and enjoy our Bible stories library. You can easily invite friends and family to join through social media, messaging apps, or directly through the app.
