Never lose a customer to a failed payment again.

1Capture ensures only users that have sufficient funds can access your free trial by making a 'smart charge' at sign-up, to temporarily withhold their first subscription payment without chargeback risks. 1Capture also lets you incentivize more users to add backup payment methods to reduce involuntary churn to near zero.

1Capture smart charge

1Capture applies a pending charge to a customer's card but as an uncaptured payment. This lets you hold the payment in Stripe's escrow for up to 7 days, without chargeback risks. If a user cancels before the end of free trial, the withheld amount is simply released back to the user.

Incentivize the addition of backup payment methods

Create and offer discount codes to users, to incentivize them to add back-up payment methods, at peak motivation periods during free trial.

Block serial disputers at signup

Unlike Chargeblast or Chargeflow, which only alert you after a dispute is filed (costing you refunds and Stripe fees), we proactively block known disputers before checkout, saving you both revenue and frustration.
