Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our 3D and AR e-commerce solutions.

How does 3D visualization improve sales?

3D visualization allows customers to see products from all angles, understand features better, and make more confident purchasing decisions, leading to higher conversion rates.

What platforms do you support?

We support all major e-commerce platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and custom solutions. Our technology works on web, iOS, and Android devices.

How long does implementation take?

Implementation typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on your product catalog size and customization requirements. We provide full support throughout the process.

Is AR technology compatible with all devices?

Our AR technology is compatible with most modern smartphones and tablets. We provide fallback options for devices that don't support AR features.

What kind of ROI can I expect?

Our clients typically see 40-80% increase in conversion rates and 60% more time spent on product pages. The exact ROI depends on your industry and implementation.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 technical support, regular updates, and maintenance services to ensure your 3D and AR features continue to perform optimally.
