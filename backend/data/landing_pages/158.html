FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How accurate are the calorie calculations?

We use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, a scientifically validated method for estimating energy needs. Combined with AMDR guidelines for macronutrient distribution, our calculations provide evidence-based recommendations. However, individual needs vary, and we recommend consulting with a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

What's the difference between the three plans?

Free plan gives you 200 credits where you can generate up to 700 meals. LIGHT gives you 2500 credits where you can generate up to 8750 meals. PRO gives you 5000 credits and 20 photo tokens where you can generate 17500 meals and 700 meals with photos.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. You can upgrade from light to pro and you will get your tokens cumulated. If you don't want to extend your paid plan, you will get your tokens one month in FREE plan and after 1 month in free plan if there is no paid plan, you will lose your tokens.

Can I exclude ingredients I'm allergic to?

Absolutely! Our ingredient filtering system allows you to specify allergens, dislikes, and preferences. The meal plans will automatically exclude these ingredients while maintaining your calorie and macro targets. This feature is available on all plans.

What happens if I don't use all my meal plans?

If you are on a paid plan and do not use all your tokens, the unused tokens will carry over for one month when you renew your subscription. This means your tokens will continue to accumulate each month as long as you stay on a paid plan. If you do not renew your paid plan, your unused tokens will remain available for one month while you are on the Free plan. After that month on the Free plan, your tokens will reset to the standard Free plan balance of 200 tokens.

Is this medical advice?

No, this is not medical advice. Our recommendations are based on general nutrition guidelines and should not replace professional medical or nutritional counseling. Always consult with qualified healthcare providers for personalized guidance.

What is your refund policy?

You can request a refund if you are not happy. All refunds will be calculated based on your token usage, so if you use all of your tokens there will be 0 refunds. If you use half of your tokens you will get 50% of your paid plan amount - (1.9% + $0.20).
