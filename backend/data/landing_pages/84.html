Android:

  1. Open an individual or group chat.
  2. Tap More options > More > Export chat.
  3. Choose Without media.
  4. Choose your desired method (ex. Message, Mail, Add to Notes) to export chat history.

iOS:

  1. Open an individual or group chat.
  2. Tap your contact name or group name > Export chat.
  3. Choose Without Media.
  4. Choose your desired method (ex. Message, Mail, Add to Notes) to export chat history.

Your chat data is stored locally in your browser and is automatically deleted when you log out, and we never store your chat data on our servers. We only send your chat data to the LLM API.
You can analyze various aspects like message frequency, word usage, active times, and participant statistics. You can also ask specific questions about your chat history, or create something out of it (like a story, or a poem, or a song, etc.)
Due to browser storage limitations, the maximum total storage for all your chats is 5MB. Each individual chat file also cannot exceed 120,000 tokens (roughly 8,000 messages or 1.5-3 months of moderate activity).
