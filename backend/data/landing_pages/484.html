Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to help you launch faster, grow smarter, and stay in control.

What makes useOrionix different from other schedulers?

Unlike bloated enterprise tools, useOrionix is built lean and fast, focused on creators, freelancers, and small teams who need to post consistently without paying $99/month. It’s affordable, intuitive, and powerful right out of the box.

Can I use it for a larger team or agency?

Yes. Our Team plan (coming soon) supports up to 5 users by default, with add-ons available. It’s designed for agencies managing multiple brands or clients, without the enterprise pricing trap.

Is the AI just marketing fluff?

No fluff here. Our smart suggestions help you reuse content, optimize posting times, and avoid duplication. It’s productivity-boosting automation, not gimmicks.

What integrations are available?

At launch, we support major platforms: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok (via API). More are coming. Let us know what you need, we’re building fast, based on real user demand.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We use infrastructure with modern security best practices, encrypted connections, token-based API auth, and no data reselling. Your content and connections stay private.

Can I customize how I manage content or clients?

Yes. You can organize posts by brand, project, or platform. Reuse media, schedule across multiple accounts, and set up custom workflows, even if you’re managing 10 clients or more.
