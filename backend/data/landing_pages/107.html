What is ComposePhotoAI?
ComposePhotoAI is an AI-powered photography application that transforms your food photos into professional restaurant-quality images. With just a few clicks, you can create stunning compositions with different styles, backgrounds, and surfaces.
How does the credits system work?
Each composition created consumes 1 credit. When you register, you receive free credits to try out the service. You can purchase additional credit packages anytime through your account.
How long does it take to process an image?
Image processing typically takes between 30 seconds to 2 minutes, depending on the complexity of your chosen composition and server traffic. You'll receive a notification as soon as your composition is ready.
Can I use the images commercially?
Yes! All images you create with ComposePhotoAI are yours and you can use them freely for commercial purposes, including restaurant menus, social media, websites, delivery apps, and marketing materials.
