Can you explain the concept of equilibrium interest rates?

ChatEDU

The equilibrium interest rate is determined at the intersection of the IS and LM curves, which represent equilibrium in the goods market and the money market, respectively.

  • The IS curve shows combinations of interest rates and output where the goods market is in equilibrium.

  • The LM curve shows combinations of interest rates and output where the money market is in equilibrium.

  • The intersection of these curves determines the equilibrium interest rate and output level ( Note ).
