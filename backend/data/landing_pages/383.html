Features

Real-time, 3D tracking of over 24,000 satellites directly in your browser. Leveraging up-to-date Two-Line Element (TLE) data and the SPG4 orbit prediction model, to offer an intuitive and immersive way to explore satellites around Earth.
Cross-Platform Satellite Tracking
Seamless real-time satellite tracking on all devices. Whether you're at home or on the go, you can monitor satellites in real-time from any web browser. Thanks to optimized performance, satellite tracking remains smooth and responsive, even with thousands of satellites displayed simultaneously.
Comprehensive Satellite Database
Access detailed satellite information including mission purpose, launch history, and current operational status. This comprehensive database enhances your satellite tracking experience by providing educational content alongside real-time tracking data, making it easier to understand each satellite's significance.
Advanced Satellite Data Visualization
Interactive graphs provide visual representation of satellite telemetry data, including altitude tracking, velocity measurements, latitude and longitude coordinates. Analyze satellite orbital patterns over time for deeper understanding of their Earth movements and trajectory predictions.
Professional Satellite Tracking Tools
Advanced search functionality by satellite name or NORAD ID, real-time satellite telemetry including velocity and altitude data, intuitive 3D navigation controls, precise mouse controls with zoom and pan functionality, and comprehensive satellite tracking features for space enthusiasts and professionals.
