The missing feature that you always wanted for your threads.

The extension adds a nice looking button called "Add Numbering" to the top of the thread UI. You'll think that it's always been there.

When you click that button, the extension adds two numbers: the index of the post and the total number of posts in the thread. So, if you have a thread with 14 posts, then the extension will add "1/14" at the beginning of your first post. And it will add "2/14" to the start of your second post. And so on.

The extension injects exactly the format of "number/number[BLANK_SPACE]". This is important to know because if you delete the blank space that's injected after the second number, then the extension won't be able to detect the numbering on the next run. Make sure to leave the format of "number/number[BLANK_SPACE]" as is.
