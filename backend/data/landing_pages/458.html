FAQ

A few things you might wonder

Are capsules private?
Yes. Capsules are private by default. You decide if anything is shared.
What can I put inside a capsule?
Words, photos, video and voice — whatever helps the moment feel real.
Can I create a capsule for someone else?
Yes. You can gift a capsule and choose when it can be opened.
Can I change the open date later?
Yes, until it’s opened. Once opened, it becomes part of that moment.
Do I have to pick a category?
No. Categories are optional — you can also create your own.
