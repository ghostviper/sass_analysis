FAQ

What photo should I upload?

Take a clear photo of your hand with your palm facing up. Make sure it’s bright, focused, and not covered by shadows.

How accurate is it?

The AI studies thousands of palm patterns to give you consistent insights. It’s based on palmistry but uses data to make it modern and reliable.

How fast are the results?

Instant! Upload your photo, make payment, and your palm reading appears within seconds.

Can I download my reading?

Yes, you can download a clean, well-designed PDF of your results right after your reading.

Is my photo safe?

Yes. Your photo is encrypted and auto-deleted after reading. We never save or share your data.

Do you offer refunds?

Since readings are delivered instantly, we can’t issue refunds. Please be sure before you submit your photo.
