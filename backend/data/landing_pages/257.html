FAQ

Will Mac Pet work on my Mac?

Mac Pet works with macOS 10.15 (Catalina) and newer versions. It's lightweight, secure, and designed specifically for the modern Mac experience.

How do I get started with my new friend?

It's super simple! Download, drag to Applications, and launch. Your new companion will appear in your menu bar instantly - no complex setup, no account required!

How does the pomodoro timer work?

Our pomodoro timer lets you set custom focus and break durations. Start a session, and your pet will help you stay on track, notifying you when it's time to take a break or get back to work!

Will it drain my battery?

Not at all! Mac Pet is incredibly efficient, using minimal resources. Your digital friend stays light and friendly to your battery life.

Can I customize my pet?

Absolutely! Choose from different pets, each with unique personalities and animations. Plus, Mac Pet adapts to your macOS accent color for a seamless experience!
