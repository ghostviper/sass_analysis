Traditional SEO targets Google's algorithm to rank blue links. AI Optimization targets Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT and Claude to ensure your brand is cited in direct answers. We focus on 'Answerability', schema depth, and semantic authority rather than just backlinks and keywords.

We provide real-time visibility tracking and optimization insights for the top 8 AI models: ChatGPT (OpenAI), Claude (Anthropic), Google Gemini, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot (Bing), DeepSeek, Meta Llama, and Mistral. We constantly update our index as new models gain market share.

No installation is required for tracking. You simply enter your domain and keywords. For optimization, we provide a dashboard of recommendations (schema changes, content structure updates) that you or your developer can implement. We also offer a WordPress plugin for automatic implementation.

We track 'Share of Voice' and 'Recommendation Rate'. Instead of a rank position (1-10), we measure how often your brand is mentioned in answers to relevant queries (e.g., 'Top 3 CRM tools'). We also track sentiment—whether the AI recommends you positively or negatively.
