Your questions, answered.

What is LegesGPT and how does it work?

LegesGPT is an AI legal assistant that helps with legal research, document analysis, and legal queries. It uses advanced machine learning algorithms trained on legal data to provide accurate and relevant legal information tailored to your specific needs.

Which jurisdictions does LegesGPT support?

LegesGPT currently supports 38+ countries worldwide, including the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, Australia, and many more. Using advanced AI algorithms, it accesses the latest legal data, including statutes, case law, and recent articles for these regions, enhanced by real-time web search capabilities to ensure up-to-date information.

Can I upload documents and images for analysis?

Yes, LegesGPT allows you to upload legal documents, contracts, and images for instant analysis. Our AI can extract key information, identify potential issues, and provide comprehensive insights from your uploaded files. This feature is available on our Plus and Premium plans.

What is the Deep Research Model and how does it benefit me?

Our Deep Research Model is an advanced AI system that can understand complex legal concepts and nuances. Unlike basic AI models, it can analyze intricate legal scenarios, conduct thorough legal research, and provide well-structured arguments with logical conclusions. This results in more accurate, comprehensive, and nuanced legal guidance for complex situations.

How does LegesGPT provide legal references?

Every response from LegesGPT includes precise citations and references to relevant laws, statutes, and case precedents. This allows you to verify information directly from authoritative legal sources and gives you confidence in the accuracy of the guidance. You can click on these references to view the original source material when available.
