Frequently asked questions

What is ChatQube?

ChatQube is an AI chatbot that helps businesses provide instant customer support, automate tasks, and boost engagement using their own knowledge base.

How can I add my AI chatbot to my website?

You just copy the ChatQube’s widget code from your dashboard and paste it into your site. Once added, the it will instantly appear on your website, ready to support your visitors.

How does ChatQube work?

ChatQube works by training its AI chatbot using your business documents, FAQs, and website pages, enabling it to deliver instant, context-aware support and keep customers engaged seamlessly.

Can ChatQube handle non-English languages?

Yes. ChatQube supports multiple languages, so your AI Chatbot can answer customers in English and many other languages based on the users language and your preferences.

How ChatQube handle multiple simultaneous customer conversations?

ChatQube is designed to manage numerous concurrent users at once. Each visitor gets instant, personalized responses without slowing down, ensuring smooth support even during high traffic.

Is there a free version of ChatQube?

Yes. You can start for free (no credit card required). The free plan gives access to all features but includes a 300 message. You can upgrade anytime for higher limits.
