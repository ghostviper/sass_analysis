Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Vidgenie.

What is Vidgenie? expand_more
Vidgenie is an AI-powered video creation platform that helps creators and businesses make professional videos in minutes. Our platform offers three main products: AI Story Videos (1-10 minute narrative videos), AI UGC Videos (authentic user-generated style content), and AI Characters (consistent custom characters for images and videos).
How does Vidgenie work? expand_more
Vidgenie uses advanced AI technology to automate the video creation process. Depending on your chosen product, our AI handles script generation, image creation, scene composition, character design, voiceovers, and more. You simply input your preferences, and our AI takes care of the rest, delivering professional-quality videos in minutes.
What types of videos can I create with Vidgenie? expand_more
With Vidgenie, you can create: 1) AI Story Videos - Long-form narrative videos (1-10 minutes) for YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram channels, 2) AI UGC Videos - Authentic-looking product videos combining AI avatars with marketing hooks and your product demos, 3) AI Character Videos - Custom character-consistent images and videos featuring your personalized AI avatar in any setting.
Do I need video editing skills to use Vidgenie? expand_more
Absolutely not! Vidgenie is designed for users with zero video editing experience. Our intuitive interface guides you through selecting your preferences, while our AI handles all the technical aspects - script writing, image creation, and video production. You get professional-quality results without the learning curve or time investment typically required for video creation.
Can I use Vidgenie for commercial purposes? expand_more
Yes, all content created with Vidgenie's paid plans can be used for commercial purposes. You own the rights to videos you create on our platform, and they can be used for marketing, social media, advertising, and more. The Free plan requires attribution to Vidgenie, while paid plans include a commercial license with no attribution requirements.
Which languages does Vidgenie support? expand_more
Vidgenie supports 29 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Hindi, and many more. This allows you to create localized content for global markets without having to hire translators or voice actors, significantly expanding your reach.
How do I get started with Vidgenie? expand_more
Getting started is easy and risk-free! Sign up for our Free plan with up to 200 credits (no credit card required). Choose the product you want to start with - AI Story Videos, AI UGC Videos, or AI Characters. Follow the simple guided process to create your first video, which typically takes just a few minutes. Once complete, you can download and share your video across social platforms or continue refining it with our editing tools.
How much does Vidgenie cost? expand_more
Vidgenie offers a free plan with up to 200 credits to get started. Our paid plans include Starter, Creator, and Pro tiers, each offering increased credit allocations and enhanced features. Each video creation action consumes credits based on its complexity. We offer both monthly and annual billing options, with annual plans providing 2 months free.
Are there any limits on team members or seats? expand_more
No, all our plans, including the Free plan, offer unlimited seats. You can add as many team members as you need without additional charges.
What is the credit system and how does it work? expand_more
Credits are Vidgenie's virtual currency used to generate videos. Different actions consume different amounts of credits based on complexity. For example, generating a 1-minute AI Story Video might use 50-150 credits depending on the AI models selected, while an AI UGC Hook + Demo video costs just 10 credits per video. Our system shows you the credit cost before generation, allowing you to make informed choices. Your monthly subscription includes a specific credit allocation that refreshes each billing cycle, with higher-tier plans offering more credits at better rates.
Do unused credits roll over to the next month or year? expand_more
Credits do not roll over from month to month on monthly plans, or from year to year on annual plans. However, on annual plans, you can use your allocated credits at any time during the year, providing more flexibility in usage. Additionally, when you upgrade to a higher tier plan or switch from monthly to annual billing, you'll automatically receive a discount based on your unused credits in your current billing period, ensuring you get full value from your subscription.
What happens if I use all my credits before the billing cycle ends? expand_more
If you use all your included credits, you can continue creating videos at the overage rate specified in your plan. Each plan has its own overage rate, with higher-tier plans offering more favorable rates (Pro plan has the lowest rate, followed by Creator, then Starter). Overage charges have a billing threshold that varies based on your currency and plan. You will be billed for overages either when you reach this threshold or at the end of your billing cycle, whichever comes first.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? expand_more
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and you'll receive a discount for any unused credits from your current billing period. You'll see this discount calculation before confirming your upgrade, ensuring complete transparency. Downgrades will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, allowing you to continue using your current plan until then.
What happens to my unused credits if I upgrade my plan? expand_more
When you upgrade to a higher tier plan or switch from monthly to annual billing, Vidgenie automatically calculates the value of your unused credits and applies a corresponding discount to your first invoice. Before confirming your upgrade, you'll see a transparent breakdown showing your current usage, the calculated discount amount, and your final price after the discount is applied. This ensures you get full value from your current subscription when moving to a higher tier.
Is there a free trial for paid plans? expand_more
We don't offer free trials for paid plans. Instead, we provide a Free plan with up to 200 credits in select geos that you can use to test our service and determine if you'd like to upgrade to a paid plan.
