Overview

Enhance your Google Calendar with a Chrome extension that adds custom colors to each event for a personalized, organized schedule.

Add More Colors to Calendar gives you the option to create any custom color for your Google Calendar! Finally, More Colors for Google Calendar! Add any color palette you would like with our Google Calendar Color Extension. How to Use: 1. After installing the extension, click the extension tab on the top bar of Chrome and then log in with your Google account to "Add More Colors to Calendar" 2. After logging into the extension, go to your Google Calendar and create an event. 3. In the event popup, you will have the option to change the color of the event. Click the color selector and then click the "+" that appears at the bottom of the default Google Calendar colors. 4. Select any color from the selector then click "Apply". 5. Save your event! Hint: You can also right-click already created Google Calendar events and change the color quicker. HOW TO CANCEL YOUR PRO SUBSCRIPTION 1. Click on the Add More Colors to Calendar logo on the top chrome bar. 2. Click the "Manage Subscription" link 3. Log in to your user account in Stripe 4. Click the "Cancel Subscription" button on the right of the screen HOW TO DELETE A COLOR 1. Click the Extension logo in the top of the Chrome browser 2. Log in if needed 3. Click the "X" beside a color you have selected 4. Delete the color. 5. You can now add a new color to replace the one that was deleted UPDATE 10/20 We have released an update of the extension to work with Chrome's new version FEATURE UPDATE 4/17 We have reduced the price of the Pro version of the extension to $5.99.
