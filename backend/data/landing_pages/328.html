What channels does Postiz support?

Postiz supports Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Reddit, Linkedin, Dribbble, Threads and Pinterest.

What kind of payment methods are supported by Postiz?

Postiz payment goes through Stripe checkout. We support any type of Visa, MasterCard, Discover credit card, and JCB. If you would like to pay with a different option, please make sure you contact support.

Can I trust Postiz?

Postiz is proudly open-source! We believe in an ethical and transparent culture, meaning Postiz will live forever. You can check the entire code / or use it for your personal use. You can check the open-source repository click here .

What is your cancellation policy?

If you have tried the product and didnt like us, contact us in the first three days, and we will issue you a full refund! You can cancel your monthly and yearly plans at any time from the dashboard. Upon cancellation, we will stop collecting payment on the next cycle.
