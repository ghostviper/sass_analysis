Got questions? We’ve got answers

Quick answers to all your burning questions about creating realistic videos with Speel.
Can I test Speel before paying?
Since we use the most advanced and realistic AI video models on the market, it's no longer feasible for us to offer a free trial. But you can sign up, create your videos and cancel anytime if it's not for you.
Which plan do I need?
It depends on how many videos you need to create. Check the pricing above, which shows you how many videos you can create, and if you need more, once you subscribe, you'll be able to add more credits.
How long are the videos?
We use 2 best-in-class video models in Speel that range from 8 to 60 seconds in video duration. If you need longer videos, you can always stitch multiple videos together, using either model.
How are you different to competitors ?
Most users move from competitors to Speel for two simple reasons: First, they say our proprietary lip sync model is much more realistic, and second, it costs less to generate each video :)
