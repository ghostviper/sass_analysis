Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our food tracking app

How accurate is the photo recognition feature?

Our AI can recognize thousands of different foods with high accuracy. For mixed meals, the system will suggest the main components and allow you to adjust as needed for maximum accuracy.

Can I track specific diets like keto or vegan?

Absolutely! You can customize your nutrition goals for any diet type, including keto, paleo, vegan, vegetarian, Mediterranean, and more. The app will help you stay on track with your specific dietary needs.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, we take data privacy very seriously. All your personal information and nutrition data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

Is the app really free or are there hidden costs?

Yes, our app is completely free to use with all core tracking features included. We believe everyone should have access to tools that help improve their health without financial barriers. We may offer optional premium features in the future, but the main functionality will always remain free.
