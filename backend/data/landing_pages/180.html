Why Glancelytics?

Built for developers who care about privacy. No complex dashboards, just the insights you need.

Anonymous Hashing

We generate a unique daily hash from visitor data (IP + UA) to count unique visitors without persistent tracking or cookies.

Privacy by Design

No IP addresses are stored permanently. No personal data is collected. Fully GDPR, CCPA, and PECR compliant out of the box.

Ultralight Script

Our tracking script is under 1KB. It supports Single Page Applications (SPAs) and won't slow down your site.
