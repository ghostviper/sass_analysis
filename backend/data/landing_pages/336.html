How it works

Stay in control
of your own workflow

Progress is designed to adapt to your workflow, not the other way around. It couldn't be simpler than this.

1.

Capture everything. Quickly add tasks, ideas, and notes to your timeline without losing focus. Everything will be there when you need it.

2.

Adapt as you go. Easily rearrange tasks, move or snooze them in the future, add subtasks, labels, and set priorities to plan your day the way you want.

3.

Leave nothing behind. Tasks automatically roll over to the next day, and the timeline will keep track of your progress. No need to keep everything in your head.
