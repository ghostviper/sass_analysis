Questions?

Frequently asked
questions

Have a question? We've got answers.
If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to us.

What is VoiceCheap?
VoiceCheap is an application that allows you to translate and dub videos in over 30 languages using voice cloning and lip-sync technology.
What are the video translation options?
Three options are available for video translation. First, direct voice cloning from the video. Second, importing audio files for voice cloning to use in the translated video. Finally, selecting a voice from our extensive library.
What languages are available for video translation?
VoiceCheap offers video translation in many languages: You can see all accepted languages here: https://voicecheap.notion.site/Language-accepted-for-VoiceCheap-6f7f09ce10fc49a186f1dd1d9c4702fb?pvs=4
What type of video can I translate?
VoiceCheap can translate any type of video, whether horizontal or vertical, short or long. We can even translate audio files. However, the maximum duration per video is 120 minutes, regardless of your subscription.
How are minutes used in VoiceCheap?
Minutes are the credit unit of VoiceCheap and are used to run features. For dubbing and translation, minutes are deducted based on your video's duration.
What happens if I don't like the service?
If you are not satisfied with the VoiceCheap service, simply send us a message and we will refund your subscription within 48 hours.
What if I don't like the result of one of my dubbings?
If you are not satisfied with the result of a video translation, simply send us a message and we will refund your minutes within 48 hours. Users with an annual subscription have priority and unlimited video refunds. Users with a monthly subscription are limited to 5 refunds per month.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time without prior explanation.
Do unused minutes roll over to the next month?
No, unused minutes do not roll over to the next month. Make sure to fully utilize your minutes during each billing cycle. Exception: for Enterprise subscriptions, minutes can roll over.
Can I use VoiceCheap on mobile?
Yes, it's possible, but we strongly recommend using a PC for the best experience and full access to all features without limitations.
