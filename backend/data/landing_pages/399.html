Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this NOT for?

This is not for dropshippers who are trying to get rich overnight. We have built this service for big brands and agencies who want to track their competition and understand their market. This requires advance computation and infrastructure and we pride ourselves on our proprietary technology.

How accurate is the sales data you track?

Our scraping technology provides very accurate data. This is achieved by progammatically requesting the add to cart function of a product to measure the inventory. This is done daily, we then measure the change in inventory with daily decreases in stock level counted as sales.

Can I track any Shopify store?

Yes! The only time we are not able to track a site is if the site owner increases the inventory level of a product on a certain day. However this happens seldom in a month and the sales trend can be tracked. Additionaly some pop up stores without actual stock will list 1000 000 products in inventory but these stores are often very new and do not go on to make sales. Established legitimate stores will manage their inventory and these are the stores we want to track.

What kind of vendor insights do you provide?

We analyze supplier performance, track which vendors are driving the most sales, monitor inventory turnover rates, and help you identify the best-performing suppliers for your business.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! You can start scraping stores immediately with our free tier. No credit card required. Upgrade anytime to access unlimited scraping, advanced analytics, and team features.

How often do you update the data?

We update product data, inventory levels, and sales information in real-time as you start tracking the site. However you will only see the sales data after midnight as we measure daily sales.

Can I export the data I scrape?

Absolutely! Export your scraped data as CSV files and create custom reports and dashboards for your team.
