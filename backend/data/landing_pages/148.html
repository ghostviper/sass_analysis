Write Without Fear.

The greatest enemy of a finished draft is the endless cycle of write, delete, rewrite. Finishdraft is built on a simple, powerful principle: momentum over perfection.

By temporarily disabling the backspace key, we liberate you from your inner editor and unlock a state of pure creative flow. The goal isn't to write a perfect draft. The goal is to finally finish one.

Writing Modes

Choose the mode that fits your session—stay flexible and in flow.

Normal Mode

A calm, distraction-minimal editor. Edit freely as you write—perfect for drafting and journaling.

Purist Mode

No backspace, no distractions. Commit to forward-only writing to build momentum and finish faster.

Typo Forgiveness

A gentle safety net—brief backspace window for quick fixes without losing your flow.

Write or Die

Playful pressure to keep typing—pause too long and the app nudges you back into action.
