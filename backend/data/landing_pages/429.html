The Story

Why I Built This

I gave up on coding.
Then my wife needed this tool.

I spent a year building random apps to learn to code. Failed at 12. Gave up.

Then my wife mentioned her data entry bottleneck: scanning handwritten job sheets, fighting terrible penmanship, typing into Excel for hours.

I looked for a tool. Google Lens? Limited. ChatGPT? 3 free images/month. Paid options? $99+/month.

So I built SupaScans: simple OCR with AI cleanup. No credit card needed to try. No subscriptions. Pay once if you want more.
