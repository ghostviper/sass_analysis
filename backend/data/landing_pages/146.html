Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we get asked.

What is FidForward?

We source the best talent, match it with your open roles and outreach to them. All in a single platform.

How fast can I get candidates for my open roles?

It takes 10-minutes to get your list of top candidates, 10-minutes to setup your outreach, and you'll start getting responses in hours. Our customers are filling their open roles in less than 7 days.

Does it work for all industries?

Yes, our search can find candidates in an industry. Typical recruitment platforms can't work beyond the traditional industries, but our AI agent researches the industry before searching.

How does billing work?

FidForward offers monthly and annual subscription plans. Payment is made through Stripe using your credit card or other secure online payment method.

Can I get a refund?

Unfortunately, we don't offer refunds. However, you can cancel your subscription at any time.

Can I try the platform for free?

Yes, you can try the Starter package for 3 days when you sign up, limited to 50 communication credits.

How do I contact support?

If you need help with FidForward platform or have any other questions, you can contact us via the contact widget in the bottom right corner of the page.

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

We offer a 20% discount for annual subscriptions. For larger accounts, please reach out to us directly via the contact widget in the bottom right corner of the page.
