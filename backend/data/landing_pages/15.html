Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? You can email us at support@aiheadshotgenerator.com or try our live chat at the bottom right corner.

What do I get?
You will receive 40+ high-quality headshots of yours via email, depending on which package you buy. They will be perfect for your LinkedIn, resume, or portfolio.
What kind of photos do I need to upload?
To get the best results, upload a minimum of 12 high-quality photos of yourself. Remember, the more photos you provide, the better. For optimal results, select a diverse range of images featuring the person in different outfits, various locations, and under various lighting conditions.
What resolution are your generated photos?
Our portrait photos have a resolution of 2048x2560 pixels, making them perfect for social media platforms and websites. For instance, platforms like LinkedIn recommend a resolution of 400x400 pixels, so our images exceed those requirements.
What do you do with my photos?
We train our AI model with input photos, render headshots then delete them and the models from our servers after 30 days.
Are my photos private?
We are fully committed to handling your data with the utmost privacy. They are stored securely on servers in the United States, by vetted, highly secure, third party partners of us.
Can I choose the suit styles and backgrounds that I like?
Yes, you absolutely can! There are many options for you to choose from, so you will definitely find those that you like.
Can I use the photos anywhere?
Absolutely! You have complete freedom to use your photos wherever you desire. We grant you a full commercial license and ownership of the photos.
Can I upload photos of other people?
Unfortunately, uploading pictures of other people is not allowed, as it violates our terms of service.
What is your refund policy?
We offer full refunds if the generated photos don't accurately represent you for various reasons. However, you are not eligible for a refund if you did not follow our upload instructions. Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase, by emailing support@aiheadshotgenerator.com or messaging our live chat. When requesting a refund, please include the email address associated with your order.
Is payment secure?
Yes, we use Stripe for payment. We do not store any of your credit card information.
