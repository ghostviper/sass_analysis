Create Personalized eCards Online with Congratz

Congratz is the perfect platform to make personalized ecards in approximately 32 seconds. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, congratulations, thank you messages, weddings, promotions, graduations, and other celebrations. Whether you need to send an ecard for a birthday, anniversary, or promotion, Congratz makes it easy. Design custom ecards with photos, GIFs, videos, and personalized messages. Share your ecard instantly.

Key Features

  • Photo uploads: Add personal photos to customize your ecard
  • GIF integration: Include animated GIFs from Giphy in your ecard
  • Video support: Embed videos from YouTube, Vimeo, and other platforms in your ecard
  • Group ecards: Build collaborative group ecards with Messages Wall feature
  • Custom messages: Personalize ecard messages and text
  • Multiple themes: Choose from various ecard templates and designs
  • Instant sharing: Send your ecard via email or shareable links
  • Affordable pricing: Starting at $0.99 per ecard

Use Cases

Congratz is ideal for remote teams celebrating milestones, families sending birthday wishes, friends congratulating achievements, businesses recognizing employee accomplishments, and anyone who wants to send an ecard quickly and easily. The platform supports both individual ecard creation and group ecard collaboration, making it perfect for team celebrations and collective messages.

How to Make an eCard

To make an ecard, users visit the Congratz website, select an ecard template or start from scratch, customize the ecard with photos, videos, GIFs, and personalized messages, and then send the ecard via email or shareable link. The entire process takes approximately 32 seconds, making Congratz one of the fastest platforms for personalized ecards online. Whether you want to design an ecard for a birthday, anniversary, or congratulations, the process is simple and fast.
